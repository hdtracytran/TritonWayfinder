Bus Services

UC San Diego offers convenient bus services to help students navigate both the campus and San Diego area. Triton Transit provides free and reliable transportation for students, faculty, and staff. In addition to campus buses, the San Diego City Transit offers easy access to off and on-campus destinations. With these bus services, commuting is convenient, eco-friendly, and affordable!
